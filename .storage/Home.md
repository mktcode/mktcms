# Welcome to the Home page 👋

Great to have you here. MktCMS is a lean, file-based CMS for self-hosted Nuxt apps — minimal, direct, and comfortable to work with day to day.

## What MktCMS gives you

- A simple admin UI with Git integration for content management
- An admin chat for editing content, questions, planning, and technical tasks
- Composables to use content smoothly in your Nuxt app
- Utilities like `sendMail` for SMTP delivery
- MDC support for modern content files

## Getting started

Just place your content in `.storage/` and use it in your application. That keeps content, files, and pages neatly in one place.

If you want to see a quick example right away, open the [PDF download](/api/content/Flyer-2026.pdf).
